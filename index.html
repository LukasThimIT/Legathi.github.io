With us you will find beautiful and practical details for setting the table, cooking, baking or decorating. We work with a number of talented factories in the Far East that manufacture our products and specialise in home and leisure.

Our collections include aprons, pot holders, tablecloths and runners, napkins, oven tins, dough bowls, cutting boards, utensils, kitchen aids and much more. We also specialise in decorative products such as cast iron candle holders, beautiful wooden games, lanterns, baskets and bags.

In addition, we make nice blankets and shawls, rugs, towels and bathrobes.

Our design studio can adapt the product to your wishes if you want to customise your order, or buy from a finished collection if you prefer. Whichever suits you best!

Contact either one of us and we will help you find what you are looking for.
